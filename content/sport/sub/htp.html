How to play

GET STARTED

Our 1-Day and 1-Week fantasy sports contests allow you to draft new lineups with each new contest.

 

Try Free Contest for Beginner

Play free contest against other beginners.

 

Draft a Lineup

Choose a sport and game set that you want to play and create a lineup. From here you can easily use the lineup to join contests of your choice.

 

THE BASICS

Enter Contest

Start by choosing a contest. Choose from one of the public contests in the game lobby. Below is our variety of public contest types.

 

Contest Types:

Free Zone - No need entry fee to join contest.

Below Top5 - Winner takes all, Top2, Top3, Top4 and Top3.

Double Up - You're prizes up to double of entry fee when you win.

Triple Up - You're prizes up to triple of entry fee when you win.

10x Up - You're prizes up to 10x of entry fee when you win.

50/50 - Finish in the top half of the field and win.

Multi Entry - Entry count are over 2 chance.

 

Draft Your Lineups

When drafting your lineup, you-re given $50,000 as a salary cap. Spend this budget on players to create your team.

Choose your players from the Player Pool. A Player Pool consists of all the players who will be in a given contest-s game set. For example: a game set for an MLB contest may include up to 15 MLB games you can select players from.

Once you-ve filled all positions in your lineup (unique to each sport) while staying under your $50,000 salary cap, you can submit your lineup into the contest.

 

Check Results

If you-ve won and placed into a paying position in a contest, the amount you-ve won, as determined by the prize structure of that contest, will be credited to your SPO-BIT account as soon as the contest ends.
